 </span> <span style="font-family: Helvetica-Bold; font-size: 18.0;text-align: left;">
     **What have we done? What brands have we launched and developed?** 

 </span> <span style="font-family: Helvetica; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     Originally partnering with Warner, Disney and Sony to create HBO Ole, the pioneer of the Pay TV industry in Latin America. 
Experience successfully partnering with global media companies to launch and grow Pay TV networks across Latin American markets.
Ole Communications is a holding company for a group of affiliated companies founded and controlled by Latin American media pioneer and entrepreneur, Enrique Cuscó. Ole Communications is a founding partner of HBO Latin America Group (HBO LAG), A&E OLE Networks, NBCUniversal Networks International Spanish Latin America and Ole Broadcasting, Ole Interactive, Playground Americas and the Actualidad Media Group. The company also coordinates and provides sales, technical, program acquisition and administrative support to affiliated companies. Ole Communications is based in Miami, Florida.
