# Ole Pictures / Foton Pictures

 <span style="font-family: Helvetica; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     #foton/olepictures
Multimedia Project Slate Financing Agreement
Ole Pictures to reach an agreement with Foton Pictures with the purpose of investing in the development, production, commercialization and management of IP and content projects in the fields of TV series, Interactive Content and Feature Films.
Investments to be limited only to projects which FP has created, acquired and developed the project and is lead producer and controlling partner.

In order to achieve these objects the parties agree to provide the following:
Foton Pictures:

 </span> <span style="font-family: Helvetica; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     * Selection and securing of 10 projects comprising the slate, which in each case can be changed for an equivalent level project before the execution of any production investments.

 </span> <span style="font-family: Helvetica; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     * Project and Talent sourcing services.

 </span> <span style="font-family: Helvetica; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     * Development and project packaging services on all projects.

 </span> <span style="font-family: Helvetica; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     * Production services on all projects.

 </span> <span style="font-family: Helvetica; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     * Business Affairs and Accounting services on all projects.

 </span> <span style="font-family: Helvetica; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     * Sales, Festival and Promotional strategy.

 </span> <span style="font-family: Helvetica; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     * Management and supervision of the investments over time as a portfolio.

 </span> <span style="font-family: Helvetica; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     
Ole Pictures to provide:

 </span> <span style="font-family: Helvetica; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     * $25,000 Monthly fee for services provided by FP

 </span> <span style="font-family: Helvetica; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     * $500,000 Development Cost Advance payable in two installments, one upon signature and the other 6 months after the first payment is made. Development advance to go

 </span> <span style="font-family: Helvetica; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     * Development Financing on a project by project basis at up to $200,000 per project.

 </span> <span style="font-family: Helvetica; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     * Production Financing on a project by project basis at up to $10,000,000 and in any casi retaining majority of the equity participation on all projects.

 </span> <span style="font-family: Helvetica; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     
Foton to recoup all development costs and any deferred development fees from the production budget of each greenlit project. 
In the case of production financing Ole Pictures will own a prorata share of what its investment represents of the entire equity pool on the project. 
In the case of a rights buyout, like is customary for streaming clients, any profits to be split between Ole Pictures 80% and Foton Pictures 20%.
