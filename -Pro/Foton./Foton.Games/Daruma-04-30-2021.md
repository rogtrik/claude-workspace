SCORING
Points can be defined by the blocks themselves, score of block should be defined the number of hit/swipes required to clear it

Mistakes can provide STARS or Multiplier… flawless is x3… one mistake x1.5.. two mistake x1.15 and three mistakes x1… we could even apply a penalty once we are past 3 mistakes

The time bar can be the time score. Defined by where the bar is t the moment the Daruma is reached.

Bonus Score can be guessing which tap or swipe gets the Daruma… all of them work but only on provides an extra point boos and it is decided ramdomly per level.


POWERUPS
Different kinds of hammers that can be earned or bought. Single use. Joker hammer can eliminate 1 block no matter the swipe.

Doppel Hammer can knock out 2 blocks but the player must use the swipe of the second block ir it will just eliminate the first one.

Chrono Hammer if used with the correct swipe, it will replenish the time bar.

Rainbow Hammer if used during Starmode will eliminate all star blocks immediately and provide 2x bonus for those blocks.
