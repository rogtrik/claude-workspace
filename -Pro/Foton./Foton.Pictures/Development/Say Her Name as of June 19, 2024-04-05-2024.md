* Foton., together with BTF agree to finance $750,000 for Production Financing into the Picture. Both parties to continue sourcing additional funding from direct sources as well as 3rd parties in order to cover the 750k of Production Financing that is still outstanding.
 
* Said Production Financing shall be split as portions of Debt and Equity, in line with the split elected by Macro for its own investment. 
	* The Debt portion, if any, shall be treated on the same terms as Macro’s loan portion of their investment as senior debt, which are currently set at 10% flat interest, with the same repayment terms. We would propose to add an early repayment clause to those terms to the benefit of the picture if agreeable to Macro. 
	* The Equity portion of the investment shall be recouped first position after senior debt plus interest is paid and to be recouped fully plus an additional, the greater of either (a) 30% ROI or (b) 12% IRR as premium on the Equity investment. 
 
* Equity Investment will represent its pro rata share of the total Equity financing for the picture and partake in its pro rata share of Net Proceeds of 50% of 100% after deferrals have been paid. Deferrals to be capped at an agreed upon amount by the Equity partners.
 
* 3% of 100% Net proceeds, with 2% going to BTF and 1% to Foton. corresponding to the production financing provided as well as continued fundraising and producing functions moving forward.
 
* 3 EP Credits, 2 for BTF and 1 for Foton. as part of the Production Financing Investment, separate and in addition to what was agreed in the Dev Funds Agreement from September 2020. 
 
* 2 Company Credits, on a shared card, in the form of “in association with BTF & Foton.Pictures”
 
* As reflected in the Dev Funds Agreement, Foton. and BTF (“Producers”) shall be entitled to a right of approval over all creative and business decisions related to the production and exploitation of the Picture; provided, however, that in the event of any impasse between Producers, on the one hand, and Investors, on the other hand, the Majority Financier’s decision shall be final.
 
* Approvals and reporting to be negotiated in good faith but on terms no less favorable than other equity investors in the Picture as reflected in Dev Funds Agreement.
 
* Investor shall be provided with four (4) complimentary invitations to any celebrity or film festival premiere of the Picture.

* Although referenced herein, this is a separate offer from Foton’s existing Dev Funds Agreement from September 2020. All terms from that document, including but not limited to economic terms, net profit participations, credits, approvals and premieres would be in place as negotiated and agreed at that time. Unless otherwise explicitly stated to the contrary, all the terms above are in addition to any and all terms reflected in the Dev Funds Agreement.
