BENDING LIGHT

CORPORATE STRUCTURE

While Foton Pictures focuses on IP and Content ownership and generation Bending Light looks at things in a much more pragmatic and objective manner.

Foton Pictures will focus on producing international theatrical films. 
It will become renown for discovering and acquiring quality source material, developing and maintaining close relationships with the top artistic and creative talent. Originating, adapting and developing unique project packages combining prestigious quality storytelling with human level relatability.

Bending Light will focus on the financing side of the content business while collecting a premium-only, owned and controlled library. 

Bending Light Structure

Ownership should be 51% Foton.Pictures and 49% pool of investors. This would at least be regarding voting right in the other option of 20% Foton.Pictures and 80% pool of investors.

Right to veto on all in-threshold project. Never to the extent of impeding in a quick and effective negotiating process.

Right to approval on out-of-threshold projects. More flexible time limits but still with the intention of not impeding the negotiating process.

Threshold to be defined by specific ratios between the cash inflow waterfall relating to the minimum guarantees and the amount to be invested in that particular project. This varies depending on whether it is equity or debt.
