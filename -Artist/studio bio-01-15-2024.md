      **bio**

 <span style="font-family: Helvetica; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     
Producer, writer, director and media executive with wide ranging multimedia experience in the international entertainment industry. He has worked at international film production companies, radio networks and pay television networks. He adapted successful international formats as well as developed and produced successful series and documentaries. In 2012 Carlos founded Foton., a content company that initially developed and produced content for clients like HBO, A&E, History, Studio Universal and E! Entertainment in Latin America. 

He has served as a member of the board of directors of Latin American AdSales and Technical Services Companies and as member of the A+E Networks, NBCUniversal and HBO Content Committees for Latin America. In 2015, Carlos shifted his focus towards the general market and film specifically which led to the creation of Foton.Pictures. He is currently the CEO of Foton. and its companies, serves as a member of the Board of Directors of Ole Communications and is the founder of Sparks.Fund



 </span> <span style="font-family: Helvetica-Bold; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     **Carlos Enrique Cuscó**
 </span> <span style="font-family: Helvetica; font-size: 14.0;text-align: left;">
     
American filmmaker, media executive, entrepreneur and philanthropist of Venezuelan and Cuban descent. Carlos is the founder and CEO of Foton., a content studio crafting stories and experiences at the juncture of content and technology. Foton. operates a venture firm that manages specialized investment vehicles executing opportunities in the media & entertainment space, a content analytics advisory business, providing insight and foresight to established media conglomerates and at its core it is an Emmy and Peabody award nominated movie, television and game production studio. 

Carlos serves as board member and content director at Ole Communications, content committee member for A+E Networks Latin America and NBCUniversal Latin America as well as the chairperson and founder of Sparks.Fund, a non-profit amplifying voices and supporting documentary filmmakers around the world. 

Carlos Enrique Cuscó recently completed “The Uninvited” which was selected to have its world premiere at SXSW 2024 and stars Pedro Pascal, Walton Goggins, Rufus Sewell and Elizabeth Reaser. The team is also in pre-production on “Violent Cases”, a feature film based on the first graphic novel created by Neil Gaiman, starring Ben Kingsley, and “Peculiar Heart”, a surrealist love story produced in partnership with Academy Award nominated director and producer, Carlos López Estrada.
